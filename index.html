Jichuan is currently a second year Ph.D. student of Civil Engineering at the University of Notre Dame, as well as a research assistant in the Brewick Group led by Prof. Patrick Brewick.

Jichuan earned his M.S. (2022) in Civil Engineering from Tianjin University. During his master program, Jichian expanded his reserach on reinforcement learning control approach to civil engineering applications as a visting master student at the Laboratory for Intelligent InFrastructure Technology (LIFT) at Nanyang Technological University in Singpore. Following his master, he spent one year as a Research Assistant in the Institute of Urban Smart Transportation and Safety Maintenance at Shenzhen University before starting his Ph.D. studies at Notre Dame.

Jichuan is a voracious learner and passionate researcher in the field of structural dynamics control, machine learning (ML)-based multi-fidelity modeling and signal processing. Jichuan has solid mechanics and control theory foundation with practical coding experiences.

Jichuan's research focus on computational methods for a range of applications. My work involves uncertainty quantification, machine learning, and high-performance computing as applied to structural health monitoring, dynamics control and wireless sensor development. Specifically, his expertise lies in the following areas:

  • Scientific machine learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Uncertainty Quantification and Bayesian Inference.
  • Multi-fidelity modeling and surrogate modeling.
  • Dynamics control with reinforcemnet learning.
  • Wireless sensor development and deployment.
